How Your Money Works

Monthly Contributions

You contribute a set amount each month. Every contribution is recorded with date and amount - you can see your complete history anytime.

Mansa-X Investments

A portion of the total fund is allocated to Mansa-X investments. Your share is tracked proportionally.

I&M Balance

The remainder stays in I&M accounts. The system automatically calculates: I&M = Total Contributions - Cumulative Mansa-X

Total Invested

All money is invested - there's no idle cash. Your total invested = Cumulative Mansa-X + I&M Balance.
