Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your investment account

How do I log in for the first time?

Your administrator will create your account and provide your email. Use the 8-digit gateway code on the login page, then enter your email and the default password: password123. You'll be prompted to change your password immediately.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Contact your group administrator to reset your password. For security reasons, password resets can only be performed by administrators. Once reset, you'll receive a temporary password that you must change on first login.

Can I change my own password?

Yes! Once logged in, go to your member dashboard and click on "Change Password." You'll need to enter your current password and then your new password (minimum 6 characters). The system will notify you when the change is successful.

Why was I locked out of my account?

The system automatically locks accounts after multiple failed login attempts to protect against unauthorized access. Contact your administrator to unlock your account. They can verify your identity and restore access.

How do I make a contribution?

After logging in, navigate to your member dashboard. Select the current month from the contributions section, enter the amount, and submit. The system will update your total immediately and record the transaction in your history.

Can I see my contribution history?

Absolutely! Your dashboard displays a complete history of every contribution you've made, organized by month and year. You can see exactly when you paid and how much, with running totals showing your cumulative investment.

What if I miss a month?

Missed months are recorded as KES 0 in the system. You can catch up by making larger contributions in subsequent months, subject to your group's rules. The system will accurately track all contributions regardless of timing.

How does the Mansa-X investment work?

Each month, a portion of the total contributions is allocated to Mansa-X investments. The system tracks:

  • Cumulative Mansa-X: Running total of all Mansa-X investments to date
  • I&M Balance: Total contributions minus Cumulative Mansa-X
  • Total Invested: Cumulative Mansa-X + I&M Balance (all money is invested)
Your share is proportional to your contributions.

How can I track my investment growth?

Your member dashboard shows:

  • Your total contributions to date
  • Your percentage share of the total fund
  • Estimated I&M balance attributable to you
  • Your portion of Mansa-X investments
  • Running total of your invested amount
All figures update in real-time.

How secure is my data?

Your data is protected by multiple security layers:

  • Encryption: All passwords are hashed using bcrypt - even administrators cannot see them
  • Gateway Code: 8-digit code required for system access
  • Session Management: Automatic logout after inactivity
  • Login Tracking: Failed attempts are monitored and can trigger account locks

What is the audit log?

The audit log is a complete record of every significant action in the system. This includes:

  • Login attempts (successful and failed)
  • Password changes
  • Gateway code updates
  • Document uploads and deletions
  • Contribution changes
  • Investment updates
Every action is timestamped and linked to the user who performed it.

Can other members see my contributions?

No. Members can only see their own contribution history and personal information. Administrators have access to all data for management purposes, but individual member privacy is protected - you cannot see other members' specific contributions.

What documents are stored in the system?

The system stores two types of documents:

  • Profile Photos: Your member photo for identification
  • MOU Documents: Legal agreements between members and the group
All documents are securely stored and can be accessed anytime.

How do I upload or update my profile photo?

From your member dashboard, click on your profile picture or the "Update Photo" button. Select an image file (JPG, PNG, or GIF) from your computer. The system will automatically resize and store it securely. Old photos are archived for audit purposes.

Can I download my MOU documents?

Yes! In the documents section of your dashboard, you'll see all your MOU documents with download links. You can save them to your computer anytime you need them. The system tracks download counts for audit purposes.

What is the gateway code for?

The 8-digit gateway code is the first layer of security. You need it to access the login page. This prevents automated bots and unauthorized users from even reaching the login screen. The code is managed by administrators and changed periodically.

Will I be notified if the gateway code changes?

Yes. When administrators update the gateway code, a notification is sent to all members' email addresses. This ensures everyone has the current code and maintains security awareness.
